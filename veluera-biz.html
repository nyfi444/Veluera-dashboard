Fulfillment SOP — Every Order
  1. Customer orders on Shopify → you receive email + Shopify app notification
  2. Log order in table above with status "Pending"
  3. Go to AppScenic/CJ Dropshipping → find matching SKU → place order with customer's shipping address
  4. Get supplier tracking number → paste into Shopify order → status auto-updates to "Shipped"
  5. Klaviyo sends shipping confirmation email automatically (set up flow once)
  6. Day 3 post-ship: Klaviyo sends check-in email
  7. Day 7 post-delivery: Judge.me sends review request automatically
  8. Update order status in log to "Delivered"

Veluera — Executive Business Plan

Mission

Veluera curates and delivers luxury furniture and home living pieces for the intentional home — making designer-quality interiors accessible without the designer price tag. We operate as a lean, AI-powered dropshipping brand targeting affluent millennials and Gen Z home buyers in the United States.

Market Opportunity

The US online furniture market is projected to reach $92B by 2027. The boneless couch category alone has grown 400%+ in TikTok searches over 24 months. High-ticket dropshipping (AOV $1,000+) yields superior margins with fewer transactions needed to hit revenue targets — one sale pays what 50 low-ticket sales would.

Competitive Advantage

  • Brand: Veluera is a luxury brand, not a commodity store — elevated copy, curated catalog, editorial aesthetic
  • AI-First Operations: 90% of content, copy, and customer service runs on AI — lower CAC, faster iteration, one-person scale
  • High-Ticket Focus: One sale = $400–$1,000 profit vs. commodity dropshipping at $5–20/sale
  • Trend + Evergreen Mix: Boneless couches (viral trending) + classic luxury furniture (evergreen demand = stable revenue)
  • Supplier Diversification: US + EU suppliers for speed, CJ as cost backup — never single-source dependent

Revenue Model

  • Primary: Dropshipping — 35–45% net margin on high-ticket furniture ($600–$2,500 price points)
  • Secondary (Month 3+): Upsell white-glove delivery, furniture protection plans, room styling add-ons
  • Future (Year 2): Private-label select SKUs (boneless couch under Veluera brand) — 60–70% margin

Marketing Strategy

  • Paid Social: Meta + TikTok ads — lifestyle creative, broad → lookalike → retarget funnel
  • Organic: TikTok + Instagram — 1 video/day, room reveal content, aesthetic reels, before/after
  • Email: Klaviyo flows — automated sequences recovering 15–25% of abandoned carts
  • Influencer: Micro-influencers (50K–500K) in home decor niche — product gifting + 10% commission
  • SEO: Blog content targeting "best boneless couch 2026", "luxury living room ideas", "high-end furniture online"

Legal & Financial Structure

  • Form LLC (single-member) immediately — separates personal + business liability
  • Open business checking (Mercury or Relay — free, startup-friendly, no fees)
  • Sales tax: register in home state, use TaxJar for auto-collection and filing
  • Retain e-commerce CPA by Month 2 — target 25–30% effective tax rate, maximize write-offs (ads, software, home office, travel)
  • Business insurance: product liability + general liability (~$500–$1,200/yr)
  • Trademark "Veluera" brand name (USPTO) by Month 3–4 — protects your brand asset

Team Structure (AI-First, One Person)

  • You: Strategy, ad buying decisions, content creation, supplier relationships
  • AI (Claude, Klaviyo AI, Tidio): Copy, email flows, customer service, product descriptions
  • VA (Month 2): Order fulfillment, basic customer replies, social scheduling
  • CPA (Month 2): Quarterly taxes, bookkeeping review, financial strategy
  • Attorney (Month 3): LLC docs, supplier contracts, trademark, refund policies
  • Media Buyer (Month 5+): Commission-based ad management at $20K+/mo ad budget

Exit / Scale Path

At $1M+ annual revenue with documented systems and 12 months of financials, Veluera becomes an acquirable asset on platforms like Acquire.com or Empire Flippers — valued at 2–4× annual profit ($2M–$8M exit potential). Alternatively: private label transition creates proprietary product line and durable brand moat that competitors cannot easily replicate.
